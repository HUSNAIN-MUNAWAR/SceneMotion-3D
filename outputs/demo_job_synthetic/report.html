Warnings and Limitations

  • Camera intrinsics are approximate. Use calibration JSON for better geometry.
  • Monocular trajectory uses relative scale only unless external scale is provided.
  • Offline-safe pseudo-depth fallback; not metric depth.

Recommended Fixes

  • Avoid pure rotation; add translation/parallax.
  • Provide a camera calibration JSON.
  • Use known scale reference, RGB-D, stereo, or IMU for metric scale.
